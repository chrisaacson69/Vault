# meta-musing
> Ideas that seem real but aren't flushed out yet — cross-domain patterns, structural observations, and dichotomies that the vault keeps running into without resolving. The tag is the organizing move: once a phenomenon is named and has a bin, future examples can accrete against it, and the accumulation itself eventually forces the shape of the idea.

## What goes here

A meta-musing is an observation that:
- Appears across multiple domains (so it's structural, not domain-specific)
- Keeps producing durable debates or conflicts that resist resolution
- Has strong intuitive pull without yet having a clean statement
- Seems to connect to several existing vault threads without being any of them

Sketch-level treatment is fine and encouraged. Don't wait for the full argument — the point is to have a place to file examples as they come.
