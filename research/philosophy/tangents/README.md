# Philosophy — Tangents
> Pages that don't fit any single trunk of the vault's philosophy but are worth keeping: surveys, author-specific analyses, and meta-rhetoric.

**Links:** [The Four Trunks](../the-four-trunks.md), [Philosophy](../README.md)

## What goes here

Tangents are pages that:
- Survey philosophical traditions without staking a vault position (e.g., a Peikoff lecture series)
- Analyze specific thinkers or commentators without fitting a trunk
- Discuss meta-rhetorical or meta-debate patterns that apply to all trunks

If a page makes a specific claim that slots into [one of the four trunks](../the-four-trunks.md), it belongs in that trunk's folder instead.
