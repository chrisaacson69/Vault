## The meta-pattern

**Voluntary cooperation → success → institutionalization → capture by incumbents → coercion → collapse → rebuild.**

Same pattern, described from three angles by three camps of thinkers.

## The three camps

- [Cyclical Historians](./cyclical-historians.md) — civilizations follow predictable patterns (Ibn Khaldun, Spengler, Toynbee, Strauss & Howe, Glubb)
- [Structural-Institutional Analysts](./structural-institutional.md) — specific decay mechanisms (Turchin, Tainter, Olson, Dalio)
- [Economic Theorists](./economic-theorists.md) — why the mechanisms operate (Hayek, Sowell, Schumpeter, Kondratiev)

## Where they agree

1. **Success contains the seeds of failure** — luxury dissolves solidarity, stability accumulates sclerosis
2. **The shift from earned authority to imposed control** is the key inflection
3. **Complexity has diminishing returns** — each additional layer costs more and delivers less
4. **External disruption is the primary reset** — stability is paradoxically dangerous
5. **Trust is the foundation; coercion destroys it**

## Nested timescales converging

All frameworks point to 2020s–2030s as a transition zone:

| Timescale | Framework | Current phase |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| ~1000 yr | Spengler | Late Civilization (Winter) |
| ~250 yr | Glubb / Dalio | Age of Decadence / Stage 5 |
| ~200–300 yr | Turchin | Disintegrative |
| ~100 yr | Strauss & Howe | Fourth Turning (Crisis) |
| ~40–60 yr | Kondratiev | Wave 5→6 (IT → AI) |
| ~120 yr | Ibn Khaldun | Gen 3–4 (luxury/decline) |
| Continuous | Olson / Hayek / Tainter | Heavily accumulated |

## Open questions

- **Is the cycle breakable?** Hayek says yes (constitutional constraints); Spengler says no. Historical record favors Spengler but n is small.
- **Does AI change the equation?** Can AI's information processing address Hayek's knowledge problem, or is tacit knowledge resistant?
- **Is Turchin's CMLS finding fatal to libertarian hope?** If cooperative institutions require external threat, does peace guarantee their decay?
- **Where are we?** Convergence or confirmation bias on 2020s–2030s?
- **Constrained-vision institutional design under AI?** Can AI preserve spontaneous order rather than replace it?
