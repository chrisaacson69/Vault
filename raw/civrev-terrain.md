## KEY MECHANIC — tiles give ONE yield type only
Unlike mainline Civ, a CivRev tile provides **food OR production OR trade, never all three**. This
forces hard city-role specialization and makes terrain mix the primary determinant of what a city
can be. (Thesis-relevant: terrain is not a minor modifier here — it's the city's identity.)

## Combat/movement notes (from the same source)
- **Roads and railroads nullify the movement cost** of all terrain.
- **Hills give an OFFENSIVE bonus** in CivRev (not only defensive as in older Civ games).
- **Siege units take no penalty attacking across rivers.**
- Defensive terrain (Forest, Hills = +50%) stacks with Walls (+100%) for city defense.
