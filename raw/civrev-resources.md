## Mechanic (grounded)
- Special resources are **permanent map landmarks**, visible to all players once the tile is explored.
- A resource's bonus is **locked until the prerequisite tech is researched** (the tech that "Makes
  Available" it). Once unlocked, it adds a flat yield to the tile for whoever works it.
- **India (Gandhi) starts with access to ALL resources** — bypasses every resource tech-gate (a
  confirmed civ bonus; strategically large).
- **Confirmed magnitude:** Game (unlock: Feudalism) = **+3 food**. (Pattern: resources give a flat
  +N of one yield, consistent with the "one yield type per tile" terrain rule.)
