# The 6 Levels of AI Engineering
From autocomplete to direct execution — where are you?

<!-- This deck was generated live by Claude Code reading vault research pages. -->

## Level 0–2: Where Most Teams Are

**L0 — Spicy Autocomplete:** AI suggests the next line. Human writes everything.

**L1 — Coding Intern:** Human architects, AI handles discrete tasks.

**L2 — Junior Developer:** AI makes multi-file changes. Human reviews all code.

> **90% of developers who think they're AI-native are at L2.**
> — Dan Shapiro, 2026

## Level 3–4: The Management Shift

**L3 — Developer as Manager:** Human directs and approves/rejects PRs. AI implements features.

**L4 — Product Manager:** Human writes specs and checks outcomes. AI implements and tests.

The human stops **writing code** and starts **evaluating results**.

## Level 5: The Dark Factory

**L5 — Spec in, software out.** No human reads the code.

| Company | Team Size | Method |
|---|---|---|
| **StrongDM** | 3 people | No sprints, no Jira, no standups |
| **Claude Code** | — | 90% written by itself |
| **Cursor** | — | $3.5M revenue/employee (5-6x SaaS avg) |

The bottleneck is not the tool — it's the willingness to redesign workflows around AI as executor.

## Level 6: Direct Execution

Software was always an **intermediate representation**.

| Level | What You Get |
|---|---|
| L5 | "Write me inventory software" → code → software manages inventory |
| **L6** | **"Manage my inventory" → agent manages inventory directly** |

The agent doesn't write the software. The agent **IS** the process.

## L6 Already Exists

- **This vault** — `/vault-ingest` doesn't write ingestion software. It ingests.
- **Algorithmic trading** — the bot doesn't generate trading software. It trades.
- **AI customer service** — the agent doesn't write helpdesk software. It helps customers.

What's changing is **generality**. LLM-based agents handle ambiguity, novel situations, and multi-step reasoning.

## The Cyborg Model

Neither side is complete alone. Distribute labor by comparative advantage:

| Human | AI |
|---|---|
| Judgment and taste | Speed and volume |
| Strategic direction | Execution and synthesis |
| Grounding in reality | Pattern recognition |
| Evaluation of quality | Generation of candidates |

**Staff+ engineers are the heaviest AI agent adopters at 63.5%** — not juniors.
