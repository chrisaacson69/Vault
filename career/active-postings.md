## Search Strategy

- **Primary boards:** LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor
- **Keywords:** "Director AI Engineering," "Director Agentic AI," "AI Enablement Director," "AI Transformation"
- **Filters:** Remote-friendly, $200K+ base, 10+ years experience accepted
- **Avoid:** Roles requiring PhD in ML/AI research, pure ML/model training roles
- **Watch for:** Forward Deployed Engineer roles at Anthropic/OpenAI (800% growth, client-facing AI implementation)
