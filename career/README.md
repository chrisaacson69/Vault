## Purpose

This vault has accumulated real, tested knowledge about working with AI — not hype, not tutorials, but hard-won lessons from building projects, running agent teams, debugging failures, and developing frameworks. The career section turns that into something demonstrable:

- **What works** — patterns, workflows, and mental models that consistently produce results
- **What doesn't** — failure modes, antipatterns, and where the hype diverges from reality
- **How to show it** — translating vault knowledge into portfolio pieces, talks, writing, and project showcases
